The Enneagram

Nine ways of paying attention to the world.

The Enneagram sorts people into nine types. Your type has less to do with what you do than with why you do it. Read it as a mirror and answer honestly about who you actually are these days. Watch for the pattern that keeps ringing true.

When you finish, read the top two or three types together. Most of us are a blend, and the runner-up can tell you as much about yourself as the type that came out on top.
